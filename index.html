About Me

With a Bachelor of Fine Arts Degree and a passion for technology, I have always been eager to find a way to marry those two interests. User experience design provides a way for me to not only combine these interests, but learn more about how people interact with thechnology. My interest in user experience design has led me to pursue a Masters of Business and Science Degree with a concentration in UXD.

code

Currently

I am currently working at Rutgers University as a web developer. I am leading a project for the largest administrative unit within the university to unify 8 individual group websites into 1 streamlined website. This project has relied heavily on knowledge of information architecture, interface design, and other user experience design skills.

Goals

What drives humans to interact with their devices the way they do? How do we explain the intensity of the human-device relationship? How do we create intutive user experiences while maintaining the ultimate aim of innovation? These are the questions I seek to answer as I launch my career as a user experience designer.
